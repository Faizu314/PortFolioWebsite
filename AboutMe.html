About Me

Hello and welcome to my portfolio, my name is Faizan Qaiser and I love creating things on a computer screen.

Who I am

I am a systems programmer, specifically for Unity C#. I love enginering solutions for the complex systems that run in video games. These can be algorithms that run in the background or at the forefront as core game mechanics.

How I think about code

I have always cared to know how programmers determine the quality of code. This drives me to research and gather as much professional opinions as I can. I am eager to understand how and why the best programmers in this field tend to write code the way they do.

How I think about Game Development

I believe the most unique aspect of game development is that it is composed of the most diverse set of skills ranging from programming to arts, music, sound, story telling, animation, design, etc. I have a good understanding of my role in this pipeline and what I have to offer.

Education
